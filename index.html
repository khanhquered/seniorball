The Senior Ball
May 11, 2019
07:30 - 11:00 PM

About



Senior Ball will be held at The Westin Copley Place on Saturday, May 11th, 2019, from 7:30pm to 11:00pm. Please bring your MIT Student ID and a government-issued photo ID or passport. Black tie attire is requested.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Ticketing



Senior Ball tickets may be purchased online.

Members of the MIT Class of 2019 can purchase up to two Senior Ball tickets. There are two ticket options: regular admission and regular admission with drink. Regular admission tickets are $50, and admission with one drink is $60. Guests must be over 21-years-old as of May 11th, 2019 to purchase drinks.

If you are unable to afford a ticket to Senior Ball, please see Miri Skolnik of Student Support Services, who will be happy to assist you confidentially.

Logistics



The Westin Copley Place is located at 10 Huntington Ave, Boston, MA 02116. The doors will open at 7:30pm and close at 10:00pm. Admission will not be allowed after 10:00pm. This is a strict cut-off, so please plan accordingly. Your guest does not have to arrive with you as long as they have their ticket. Anyone without a ticket will not be admitted to the event.

There will be no coat or bag check. Large bags and backpacks are not allowed, but small purses and bags are permitted. Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside and will be confiscated at the door. Please plan accordingly!

Readmittance is not allowed.

Refreshments



Light refreshmensts, small bites, and desserts will be served. Please plan to eat dinner beforehand.

Attire



The event is black tie, but of course feel free to interpret that as you wish. We can’t wait to see you all at Senior Ball!
